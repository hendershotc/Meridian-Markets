🚀 Get Started with Alpaca Paper Trading

1
Sign up for a free account at alpaca.markets — no credit card needed for paper trading
2
In the top-left of your Alpaca dashboard, switch to Paper Trading account, then go to API Keys and generate a key pair
3
Install the SDK: pip install alpaca-py
4
Add to your .env file:
ALPACA_API_KEY=your_paper_api_key
ALPACA_SECRET_KEY=your_paper_secret_key
ALPACA_PAPER=true
5
Restart the app and come back here — the bot will connect automatically
